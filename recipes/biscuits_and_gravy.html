Biscuits and Gravy

Description

This recipe is for a delicious breakfast, made from biscuits topped with a thick sausage gravy for a great start to the day. A bit of extra calories never hurts!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted
  • 16 oz pork breakfast sausage
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 12 fluid oz evaporated milk
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F + Grease a baking sheet.
  2. Whisk 2 cups flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt together in bowl. Cut shortening into flour mixture with a pastry cutter until mixture is crumbly. Mix in egg and milk until incorporated. Turn dough out onto a well-floured work surface and knead for 1 minute.
  3. Roll dough out to a thickness of 3/4-to-1-inch thick, cut with a biscuit cutter, and place biscuits onto the prepared baking sheet. Brush tops of biscuits melted butter.
  4. Bake in preheated oven until biscuits are golden brown, roughly 8 to 10 minutes.
  5. Cook and stir sausage in large skillet over medium heat for about 10 minutes. Drain excess grease. Stir 1/4 cup butter into sausage until butter has melted and mix in 1/4 cup flour until thoroughly combined. Pour evaporated milk and water into sausage mixture, bring gravy to a simmer, and reduce heat to low;simmer gravy until thickened, about 5 minutes. Season gravy with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve and enjoy.
