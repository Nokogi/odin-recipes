Meatballs

Description

These meatballs are a fantastic protein source, with a delicious sauce coating them to ensure you get plenty of flavour exploding in your mouth with every bite!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound ground veal
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1 cup grated romano cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons chopped italian flat leaf parsley
  • salt and ground pepper to taste
  • 2 cups stale italian bread, crumbled
  • 1 1/2 cups lukewarm water
  • 1 cup olive oil

Steps

  1. Combine beef, veal, and pork in large bowl. Mix in cheese, eggs, garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add bread crumbs and slowly mix in water, 1/2 cup at a time, until a mixture is moist but still holds its shape. Shape into meatballs.
  3. Heat olive oil in large skillet; add meatballs in batches and cook until browned on all sides, slightly crisp and cooked through. Drain on paper towels.
  4. Enjoy!
