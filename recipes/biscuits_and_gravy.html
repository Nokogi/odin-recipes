Biscuits and Gravy

Description

This recipe is for a delicious breakfast, made from biscuits topped with a thick sausage gravy for a great start to the day. A bit of extra calories never hurts!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted
  • 16 oz pork breakfast sausage
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 12 fluid oz evaporated milk
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
